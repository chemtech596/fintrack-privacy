Privacy Policy for FinTrack

Last updated: March 2026

FinTrack is a personal expense tracking application designed to help users manage daily and monthly spending. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the FinTrack mobile application.

By using the FinTrack app, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

---

1. Who Can Use FinTrack

FinTrack is designed for general users who want to manage their personal expenses and budgets. The application can be used by individuals, students, families, or anyone who wants to track and manage their spending.

FinTrack is not specifically directed toward children under the age of 13.

---

2. Information We Collect

FinTrack may collect limited information necessary for the functioning of the app.

This may include:

• Basic account information through Firebase Authentication
• Expense data entered by users such as categories, budgets, and transaction amounts
• Technical information required for app functionality

The app does not collect sensitive personal information such as bank details, credit card numbers, or government identification numbers.

---

3. How We Use the Information

The information collected is used only to provide the core functionality of the application, including:

• Storing expense records
• Synchronizing data across sessions
• Managing user budgets and categories
• Improving the performance and reliability of the application

We do not sell, rent, or trade user data to third parties.

---

4. Firebase Services

FinTrack uses Google Firebase services for backend functionality such as:

• User authentication
• Secure database storage
• Data synchronization

Firebase may collect certain technical information as described in Google's Privacy Policy.

Google Privacy Policy:
https://policies.google.com/privacy

---

5. Camera Permission

The camera may be used for QR code scanning related to UPI payments.

The application does not store, record, or upload camera images.
Camera access is used only to scan QR codes and initiate payment requests.

---

6. Microphone Permission

FinTrack may use the microphone for voice-based expense entry.

Example voice command:
"Add grocery 200"

Voice input is processed using the device's speech recognition service and FinTrack does not store or record audio data.

---

7. Internet Access

The application requires internet access to:

• Connect to Firebase services
• Synchronize user expense data
• Ensure secure data storage

---

8. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect user data. User information is stored securely using Firebase cloud services and standard security practices.

However, no method of electronic storage or transmission over the internet is completely secure.

---

9. Data Sharing

FinTrack does not share personal user data with third parties except when required for core services such as Firebase infrastructure.

---

10. Children's Privacy

FinTrack is not specifically designed for children under the age of 13. The application does not knowingly collect personal information from children.
